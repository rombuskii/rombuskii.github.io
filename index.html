Intro

Hey, welcome to my workshop. I'm Eruba, and I'm currently pursuing a Bachelors of Science in Computer Science at Northeastern University. I'm originally from Lagos, Nigeria. Born and raised with my two older brothers. As young as I could remember I've always had a fascination for technology. From back in the day, when Nokia's ruled the world and Bounce or Snake were the hottest games you could download on a phone. Or when my brother brought home a Gameboy Advance for his birthday with Pokemon Yellow and we would all gather arounds sinking countless hours into the game. Over the years, I've dabbled in many fields of computing: Gaming, Web Design, Cybersecurity, Media, Database Design and more. Each experience made my journey learning richer and more exciting. Hopefully, this workshop imparts that same feeling into you. Here's a sample of some awesome stuff I've worked on!
